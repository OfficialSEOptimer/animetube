About the Project

NamiTube was born from a simple idea: animation deserves a dedicated, premium streaming platform. While other sites mix live-action with animated content, we focus exclusively on what we love most — anime, cartoons, animated movies, and series.

Powered by the TMDB API, NamiTube provides accurate metadata, high-quality posters, and comprehensive information about every title in our library. We don't host any content ourselves; instead, we aggregate embeds from trusted third-party providers to give you seamless access.

Our platform is designed to be fast, responsive, and beautiful across all devices — from mobile phones to ultrawide monitors. Every pixel, every animation, and every interaction has been carefully crafted to deliver a Netflix-level experience for animation enthusiasts.

This is an open-source, community-driven project. We believe in transparency, user privacy, and the power of shared passion for great animation.
